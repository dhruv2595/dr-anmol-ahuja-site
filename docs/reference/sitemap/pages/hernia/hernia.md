### Hernia Surgery Delhi Noida

**About Hernia**

Hernia is a very common problem that is quite prevalent in children and elderlies. It is marked by the formation of a localised bulge in the abdominal or the groin area.

Hernias result from weakness or perforations in the peritoneum, creating gaps through which organs may protrude. Initially patients experience slight swelling that disappears when lying down, gradually becoming more prominent.

**Various types**

Hernias divide into Internal and External categories.

**Internal Hernia**

These are very rare and usually hard to detect. These occur when an organ in the abdominal cavity protrudes through the wall of another organ thereby causing immense pain and discomfort.

**External Hernias**

- Inguinal hernias (75% of cases)
- Femoral hernias (more common in females)
- Incisional hernias (post-surgery)
- Umbilical hernia
- Epigastric hernia
- Divarication of Recti

**Some common symptoms**

- Localised swelling or bulge
- Pinching pain and dull ache
- Persistent pulling sensation
- Change in bowel habits
- Back Pain

**Proper prognosis**

Hernia cannot be cured by the used of oral medication and needs to be treated surgically.

**Common surgeries for inguinal hernia:**

1. Laparoscopic TAPP
2. Laparoscopic TEP
3. Enhanced TEP
4. Open Lichenstein mesh repair
5. Tissue repair
6. Emergency surgeries

**For ventral hernia:**

1. Laparoscopic IPOM Plus
2. Laparoscopic TAPP Plus
3. Component Repair Anterior/Posterior
4. E-TEP RS
5. E-TEP TAR
6. Scola
