### Definition
"Common bile duct stone, also known as choledocholithiasis, is the presence of gallstones in the common bile duct (CBD)"

### Symptom Summary
The most frequent complaint involves "upper abdominal pain on the right side of the body, where the liver and gallbladder are situated."

### Associated Complications
- Nausea, fever, chills
- Jaundice (yellowing of skin/eyes)
- Dark urine and itching
- Digestive issues (greasy stools)
- Potential infections like cholangitis

### Diagnostic Methods
Blood tests, abdominal ultrasound, CT scans, ERCP, and MRCP procedures

### Treatment Options
1. ERCP
2. Laparoscopic common bile duct exploration
3. Open common bile duct exploration
